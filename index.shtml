expectations & reminders

There are a few things I want to make sure everyone knows:

  • After week 2 - I do not want to help you with your FTP. This is a non-negotiable skill you should know as a web developer
  • Spaces, special characters and capital letters should never be used in file or folder names on the web. The habit might be easy to get into on your desktop, but as someone who crafts web pages, you should avoid this.
  • The student webspace is: students.thenet.ca/(student_name) ← Notice that there is no www at the front
  • You will be maintaining a web site for this course. A web development course. Think about that a moment.
  • The audience for your course homepage is your instructor, design it for me:
    • Have your assignments easy to find
    • The course is delivered weekly, so should your work
    • Use techniques you have learned in class
    • You don't have to spend hours making it beautiful - that is not an aim of this course. Focus on making it usable and befitting of the content
